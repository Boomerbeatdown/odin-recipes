Homemade Hamburger Helper

Description

This homemade hamburger helper is far better than the boxed mix. With fresh spices, and a hint of tomato, it's beefy, cheesy, savory, and has all the makings of a weeknight family favorite.

Ingredients
  • 1 pound of beef
  • 1/2 cup diced yellow Onion
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 1/2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup macaroni
  • 6oz cheddar cheese
Steps
  1. gather Ingredients
  2. cook beef and onion until beef is brown
  3. add tomato paste,ketchup,garlic powder,chili powder,salt,paprika, and pepper
  4. add broth and noodles and cook until noodles are tender
  5. remove from heat and add cheddar cheese
